About Us

Dance is one of the most expressive crafts known to mankind. People of all ages and preferences admire dance and, people in India are no different. In our country, there is a song and dance for virtually every season and reason.

The Let's Naach Dance Academy was set up in 2010 to teach dance along the lines of danceinstitutes overseas. Currently its curriculum consists of styles such as Jazz, Classical Ballet, Contemporary, Urban and Funk.Today, The Let's Naach Dance Academy has a huge student database spanning New Delhi, NCR and Mumbai. Our Company offers MASTER TEACHERS for regular classes,private lessons, workshops, performances & intensives across India in virtual mode.
